Instruction Manual: Entering New Materials

Note: This manual provides step-by-step instructions for adding new consumable materials to the inventory system.

Overview

Adding new materials to the inventory system is essential for tracking stock levels and managing reordering. This process allows you to define important details such as item name, type, location, and reorder thresholds.

Step 1: Access the Materials Entry Page

From the home page, click on the "Consumable Materials" card, then click the "Add Material" button at the top of the page.

Step 2: Enter Basic Material Information

Fill in the following required fields:

  • Item Type: Select the appropriate category for the material
  • Item Name: Enter a descriptive name for the material
  • Item Short Code: Enter a brief code for quick identification
  • Normal Location: Select where the item is typically stored

Step 3: Enter Quantity Information

Specify the quantity details:

  • Whole Quantity: Enter the current stock level
  • Reorder Threshold: Set the minimum quantity at which to reorder

When stock falls below the reorder threshold, the item will be highlighted in the materials list.

Step 4: Enter Additional Details (Optional)

You may also provide:

  • Item Description: Detailed information about the material
  • Units (Whole): The unit of measurement for whole items (e.g., "box", "case")
  • Units (Part): The unit for individual parts (e.g., "sheet", "piece")
  • Parts Per Whole: How many individual parts make up one whole unit

Step 5: Submit the Form

Review all entered information for accuracy, then click the "Submit" button at the bottom of the form. If successful, you will be redirected to the materials list where your new item should appear.

Tip: Items that need reordering (below threshold) will be highlighted in yellow in the materials list.

Field Descriptions

Required Fields

  • Item Type: Category of the material (e.g., Paper, Ink, Tool)
  • Item Name: The full name of the material
  • Item Short Code: A brief identifier code
  • Normal Location: Where the item is typically stored
  • Whole Quantity: Current stock level

Optional Fields

  • Item Description: Detailed description of the material
  • Reorder Threshold: Minimum quantity before reordering is needed
  • Units (Whole): Unit of measurement for whole items
  • Units (Part): Unit of measurement for individual parts
  • Parts Per Whole: Number of parts in one whole unit
Important: Once a material is added, you can edit its details later by clicking the "Edit" button next to the item in the materials list.
